Our journey in this organization

We have created this organization with the sole purpose to help the people in these troubling times of Covid-19 pandemic and to provide help to as many people as we can inorder to create opportunities for them to learn and grow.
And to make sure that Covid 19 doesn't pose as a hurdle in anyone's daily life.

April 2020

The foundation of our organization and the beginning of us helping out the people by creating an awareness for Covid-19 while also providing our facilities to the masses.

June 2020

Our organization had a huge growth thanks to the several partners and companies that partnered with us. This helped us reach out to even more people and spread our help.

September 2020

We have managed to touch new heights and have more than 1,00,000+ users actively using our website using facilities.
